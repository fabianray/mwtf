About Us

Magic Wolf Tree Farm is a small family owned nursery located in Mexia, Texas that specializes in native grown ball and burlaped trees. Owned and operated by Gerado Garcia who has been in the business for over 30 years, his goal is to provide his customers with unique and quality trees. When selecting the trees, he ensures that they have a full spread as well a solid root system to give them the highest chance of survival.

Ball & Burlap

All our B&B trees where grown naturally in fields from seeds. We searching the fields for trees we unsure we select only healthy trees that have branches all around to give it a nice full appearance, trimming any unwanted growth. Each tree is machine dug and placed in a wired basket lined with burlap. We then tighten the basket with rope to ensure the soil and roots are not lose. The trees are then cared for until they are ready to be sold and delivered.

Container Trees

For our smaller tree specimens, such as the Crepe Myrtle and Vitex or Chaste Tree, we grow in small containers. We transplant the trees into larger containers when they are ready during this time we prune the roots to prevent spiraling of the roots.

Farm Grown

We have also recently started growing our own trees on a 10 acre land in Wortham, Texas. With over 1000 trees and counting, we will soon be able to supply our customers year round.
